Part A:  Modernization  of  the Borrower's  Accounting Information

Standards

Development  and issuance  of  internationally accepted private
sector  accounting  and  auditing  standards,  and  establishment  of
enforcement  mechanisms  for  their utilization.

The  Project  is  expected to  be  completed by January 31,  2000.
